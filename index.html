About Us

Ref Tec was formed through the merging of two ncompanies, Hi-Tec Refrigeration & Intercape Refrigeration.

Hi-Tec was formed in 2002, complimented by 16 years experience in industrial refrigeration. It’s expertise included commercial & secondary refrigeration systems which also involved the design and construction of cold & freezer rooms. Hi-Tec’s vast knowledge of electronic control systems as well as refrigeration alarm management is an indication of it’s commitment keeping up to date with the most recent technology the industry has to offer.

Intercape Refrigeration, driven by service delivery and spanning 31 years in the commercial sector.

Ref Tec incorporates the best of both companies offering a more dynamic approach.

Its innovative ideas combined by an excellent service delivery makes Ref Tec a must for any business in the refrigeration industry. We distance ourselves from lateral thinking, allowing us to see a much bigger picture… our plants are equipped with the latest in CRii technology allowing us to run our systems down to 10% capacity.

The savings in energy consumption is phenomenal…..
